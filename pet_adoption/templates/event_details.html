Annual Pet Adoption Expo

Join us at the Annual Pet Adoption Expo on May 15, 2024, held at Central Park, 456 Oak Street, City, from 11:00 AM to 5:00 PM. This highly anticipated event is a celebration of compassion and companionship, bringing together animal lovers and adorable pets in search of their forever homes. Whether you're looking to adopt a new furry friend or simply enjoy a day surrounded by animals, the Annual Pet Adoption Expo offers something for everyone. Meet a diverse array of animals, from playful puppies to affectionate cats, and discover the joy of pet adoption. Engage with representatives from local shelters and rescue organizations, who will be on hand to provide information about adoption procedures, pet care, and volunteer opportunities. Enjoy live entertainment, educational presentations, and interactive activities for the whole family, all while supporting the lifesaving work of animal welfare organizations.

The Annual Pet Adoption Expo is more than just an adoption event—it's a community gathering dedicated to making a difference in the lives of animals in need.

By attending, you'll not only have the chance to find your perfect pet companion but also contribute to the larger mission of ending pet homelessness. Whether you're a longtime pet owner or considering adoption for the first time, this event offers a welcoming and inclusive environment where everyone can come together to celebrate the bond between humans and animals. Don't miss this opportunity to be a part of something truly special and help make dreams come true for shelter pets awaiting their forever families.

Furry Friends Fun Run

Lace up your sneakers and join us for the Furry Friends Fun Run on June 20, 2024! Held at Riverside Trail, 789 Maple Avenue, City, starting at 9:00 AM, this exciting event combines fitness, camaraderie, and a whole lot of furry fun. Whether you're a seasoned runner, a casual jogger, or simply love spending time outdoors with your four-legged companion, this event is perfect for all ages and fitness levels. Embark on a scenic journey along the riverside trail, surrounded by lush greenery and the company of fellow animal enthusiasts. Bring your dog along for the run and enjoy bonding time while staying active and healthy together.

The Furry Friends Fun Run isn't just about crossing the finish line—it's about celebrating the special bond between humans and their pets. After the run, stick around for post-race festivities, including pet-friendly activities, vendor booths, and delicious refreshments. Whether you're participating as a solo runner, a team, or with your furry running buddy, you'll be supporting a worthy cause with every step. Proceeds from the event go towards animal welfare initiatives, including rescue, rehabilitation, and adoption efforts. Join us for a day of fitness, friendship, and furry companionship, and help make a positive impact on the lives of animals in need.
