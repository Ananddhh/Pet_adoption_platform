Annual Pet Expo and Adoption Fair

Save the date for the Annual Pet Expo and Adoption Fair on September 20, 2024, where pets and people come together for a day of fun and furry companionship! Explore a vibrant marketplace featuring pet supplies, accessories, and treats from local vendors, and discover the latest trends in pet care and wellness. From interactive demonstrations to informative seminars, there's something for every pet enthusiast at the Annual Pet Expo.

The highlight of the event is the pet adoption area, where you can meet adorable animals from shelters and rescue groups in search of loving homes. Whether you're looking to add a new member to your family or simply want to support a worthy cause, you'll find plenty of furry friends waiting to capture your heart. Engage with animal experts, participate in pet contests, and enjoy live entertainment throughout the day. By attending the Annual Pet Expo and Adoption Fair, you're not only celebrating the joy of pet ownership but also making a positive impact on the lives of homeless pets in your community. Join us for a day of wagging tails, happy hearts, and endless opportunities to connect with pets and pet lovers alike.
