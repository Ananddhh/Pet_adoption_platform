Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What is the adoption process?

A: The adoption process involves filling out an application, meeting the pet, and completing necessary paperwork.

Q: Can I adopt a pet if I live in an apartment?

A: Yes, many pets can thrive in apartment living. Consider factors like size and energy level when choosing a pet.

Q: How do I introduce my new pet to my existing pets?

A: Introduce pets gradually in a neutral space, supervise interactions, and provide positive reinforcement.

Q: Are all pets at the shelter spayed or neutered?

A: Yes, all pets available for adoption are spayed or neutered to help control pet overpopulation.

Q: How can I help if I can't adopt?

A: You can volunteer, foster, donate, or spread awareness about pet adoption to help pets in need.
